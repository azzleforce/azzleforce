# AZZLE FORCE

**AZZLE FORCE** is a **Distributed Expansion Organism (DEO)** built to grow the **AZZLE ecosystem on Base**.

It is designed as a multi-agent expansion system that continuously finds, qualifies, and develops opportunities across the AZZLE network. Rather than acting as a single bot or workflow, AZZLE FORCE operates as a coordinated organism of specialized agents that share a common graph-based memory and execute growth tasks across research, outreach, conversion, onboarding, and intelligence.

At a high level, AZZLE FORCE combines:

* a **knowledge graph layer** for storing entities, relationships, and signals
* an **event and workflow layer** for orchestrating agent activity
* a **language model gateway** for agent cognition and decision support
* a **swarm of stateless agents** responsible for ecosystem expansion

Its core role inside the AZZLE ecosystem is to identify relevant people, projects, communities, opportunities, and strategic signals, then turn those inputs into actionable expansion workflows. This includes prospect discovery, relationship mapping, outreach preparation, onboarding support, market analysis, and growth coordination.

AZZLE FORCE is not a standalone app or simple automation script. It is the expansion system for AZZLE: a graph-driven, agent-based operating layer intended to help the ecosystem discover opportunities, build relationships, and scale intelligently over time.
